Armour Types

Infantry Low

  • Typically used by builders, with a few notable exceptions.
  • Anti-Tank weapons are often effective against it
  • Certain Jumper units do less ranged damage to it.
  • Jumper squad leaders often improve the squad's ability to kill builders

Building Low

  • Typically used by Listening Posts and Power Generators
  • In other words, knowing what units are effective against building_low helps with destroying the enemy's economy.
  • Flamers do enhanced damage against them. However, they have low range. This promotes tactical risk vs reward.
  • Many units do better in melee against building_low than using ranged. This further promotes tactical risk vs reward.

Heavy Infantry Medium

  • Weak against plasma weapons
  • Also known as Space Marine Armour
  • Many races have access to this armour type
  • Resistant to heavy bolters, though other armour types provide better protection

Heavy Infantry Low

  • Typically used by Tau. Kasrkin also use it.
  • Typically weaker in melee, compared to Heavy Infantry Medium
  • Superior protection against Heavy Bolters
  • Superior protection against area of effect spells
  • Like Heavy Infantry Medium, this armour type is weak against plasma weapons

Heavy Infantry High

  • Also known as Terminator armour
  • Resistant to plasma weapons, compared to Heavy Infantry Low and Medium
  • Often units with this armour type morale break before they die

Vehicle_High

  • Used by Land raiders, Monoliths, Baneblades, Wraithlords, Tomb Spyders
  • Even more resistant to anti-tank weapons
  • Commanders often do enhanced damage in melee to them
  • With the larger vehicle_high units, it is often best to stun them with various forms of EMP and then trap them with dreadnoughts or other uber melee units
